Generations



There are currently eight generations of Pokémon. A generation is based on the region the game is based in and the specific Pokémon within that region.



Kanto

The Kanto region is the very first region created in the Pokémon franchise and was based of Kanto, Japan. This region had brought the first 151 Pokémon. This region was used in the original games Red, Blue, Yellow, and Green (Green only being released in Japan). This region is very much loved by the players through nostalgia and has been used in many remakes of the games such as Firered, Leafgreen, Let’s Go Pikachu, and Let’s Go Eevee. It was also brought back to accompany the Johto region in the second generation of games in Gold, Silver, Heartgold, and Soulsilver.
