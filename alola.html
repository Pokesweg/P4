Generations



There are currently eight generations of Pokémon. A generation is based on the region the game is based in and the specific Pokémon within that region.



Alola

The Alola region is the seventh generation created in the franchise and was based off Hawaii, USA. This region had brought 88 Pokémon (722-809). This region added the feature of Z-moves which was an extra move that could be done given the Z-ring and Z-stones. This region was also the first region to break the normal “Gym Leader challenge” and added trial captains. This region was used in the games Sun, Moon, Ultra Sun, and Ultra Moon.
